CASE STUDY

Shef-LMS Learning Management System

A comprehensive e-learning platform designed to elevate development skills with interactive courses, progress tracking, and video streaming.

E-Learning Video Streaming Analytics
Visit Live Site Build Similar Project
Client Shef Education
Industry EdTech / E-Learning
Duration 4 Months
Platform Web Application

The Challenge

Shef Education needed a modern learning management system that could compete with established platforms while providing unique features. The challenges included:

  • Building a scalable video streaming infrastructure
  • Creating an engaging, interactive learning experience
  • Implementing progress tracking and certificates
  • Payment gateway integration with subscription models
  • Admin dashboard for course management
  • Mobile-responsive design for learning on-the-go

Our Solution

We developed a feature-rich LMS with focus on user experience and scalability:

  • AWS CloudFront CDN for fast video delivery worldwide
  • Interactive video player with notes, bookmarks, and speed control
  • Automated certificate generation with verification
  • Razorpay & Stripe integration with subscription management
  • Comprehensive admin panel with analytics dashboard
  • PWA support for mobile-first learning experience

Features We Built

Complete learning management solution

Video Streaming

Adaptive bitrate streaming with HLS support for smooth playback on any device and network speed.

Course Management

Easy-to-use admin panel for creating courses, chapters, lessons, and quizzes with drag-and-drop interface.

Progress Tracking

Detailed progress analytics for students showing completed lessons, quiz scores, and learning streaks.

Certificates

Auto-generated, verifiable certificates upon course completion with unique certificate IDs.

Payment Integration

Multiple payment gateways with subscription plans, coupons, and refund management.

Discussion Forum

Course-specific discussion forums for student interaction and instructor Q&A.
